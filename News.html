Ancient Pottery Found in Eastern Rwanda

Archaeologists discovered ancient pottery believed to be hundreds of years old. This discovery helps us understand early settlement patterns in Rwanda.

Stone Tools Discovery

Stone tools found near a historical site show evidence of early human activity and craftsmanship. These discoveries help archaeologists understand how early communities lived and adapted to their environment.

The Urewe Culture

The Urewe culture settled Rwanda during the first millennium BC, introducing iron smelting and pottery. Their decorative ceramics and farming practices mark the earliest Iron Age civilization south of the equator.
